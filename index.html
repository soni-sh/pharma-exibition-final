About us

Organiser

Exhibitors Connect Global is an innovative exhibition company that offers fresh perspectives on creating opportunities through exhibitions and conferences. It aims to foster interactions among pharmaceutical companies in India and provide them with a platform to enhance their business potential.

By organizing exhibitions, conferences, and technical sessions, Exhibitors Connect Global connects key stakeholders in the pharmaceutical industry, including decision-makers, associations, and consultants.

Advisory Board Panel

Our Advisory Board at World of Pharma includes Visionary Entrepreneurs, Pharma Industry Professionals, Innovation Catalysts, Stockists, Distributors, Retailers and Government Institutions. They play a pivotal role in enhancing and excellence within the Pharmaceutical industry. This board is instrumental in guiding WOPHEX to better understand industry dynamics and bridging the gap between suppliers and brands within our ecosystem.

The WOPHEX Advisory Board will provide invaluable mentorship and guidance, leveraging their expertise to enhance our understanding of industry nuances and optimize our ecosystem's offerings.
